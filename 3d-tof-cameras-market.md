<p><strong>The 3D TOF Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Time-of-flight Camera</li><li>CCD Time-of-flight Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera market is primarily categorized into CMOS and CCD types. CMOS Time-of-Flight cameras leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and compact designs, making them ideal for consumer electronics and mobile devices. In contrast, CCD Time-of-Flight cameras utilize charge-coupled device technology, delivering superior image quality and sensitivity, which is beneficial for industrial applications and high-end imaging. Both types are essential for applications such as robotics, automotive, and security systems.</p></p>
