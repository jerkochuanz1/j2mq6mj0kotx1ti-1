<p><p>The prebiotic food market comprises several types, including functional oligosaccharides, polysaccharides, polyol, and others. Functional oligosaccharides, such as inulin and fructooligosaccharides, promote beneficial gut bacteria. Polysaccharides, like resistant starch, contribute to gut health and digestion. Polyols, including sorbitol and mannitol, provide sweetness and act as prebiotics. The "others" category includes various emerging ingredients offering prebiotic benefits. Together, these segments address rising health awareness and demand for gut-friendly foods, driving market growth.</p></p>
